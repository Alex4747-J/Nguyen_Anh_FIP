Romantic Getaway

Spring is in the air and life in Nature is coming alive with excitement here at Algonquin Park, a perfect time to take a romantic vacation at Couples Resort and visit Algonquin Park.

Weather here in the spring can often be several degrees warmer than if you live near places like Lake Ontario as the ground here can warm up fast and we have no big Lake effect keeping air cool. Here you can enjoy outdoor hiking plus many other sports and attractions.
